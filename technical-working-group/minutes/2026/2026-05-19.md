- dev-docs job is ready to be merged.
- Someone at ISIS will do the macOS job in the next couple of weeks.
- An issue to migrate the nightly build job will be added.
- The 'Combined workflow with dependency graph' (#39497) issue is no longer required - closed.

- The CppCheck version update will wait until next release.
